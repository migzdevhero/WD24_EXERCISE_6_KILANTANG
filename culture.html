Kadaugan sa Mactan (or the Victory in Mactan) is a celebration reenacting the victory of Mactan people (Oponganons) during the famous “Battle of Mactan” in the 16th century.

Every year, the town people celebrate the Feast Day of the Virgen dela Regla (or Virgen de Regla) during the 20th and 21st day of November. The novena (which starts on November 12), is one of the main highlight of the celebration..

The choice of restaurants on Mactan Island is excellent, covering a wide range of cuisines and budgets. You don't have to look far when it comes to quality food as all the high-end hotels have superb kitchens. Many also have a choice of restaurants, often including Italian, Chinese, and seafood.

There are three ways to get around Mactan; by jeepney, tricycle, and taxi. There are no public utility buses that ply Mactan, only private buses of hotel and resorts used to shuttle their guests around the island.
